Mirror App Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2024

Information We Collect

Mirror collects:

  • Mood ratings and personal reflections you enter
  • App usage data for analytics and improvement
  • Device information for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Generate personalized AI insights about your journaling habits and reflection themes/li>
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage

Your personal reflections are stored locally on your device. Analytics data may be processed by third-party services including Firebase and Meta Audience Network for advertising.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal reflections. Anonymous usage data may be shared with analytics providers to improve the app.

Your Rights

You can export or delete your data at any time through the app settings.
